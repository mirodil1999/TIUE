About TIUE

The Tashkent International University of Education (TIUE) is a private educational institution founded in 2020 and licensed by the Government of the Republic of Uzbekistan thereof. The creation of the TIUE was based on a guiding principle and a belief that greater access to a more diversified education will contribute to a greater social justice.
The goal of providing greater access to education will be realized through a four-pillar system based on both private and public-private partnership models.

Our Vision

Tashkent International University of Education will become a purposeful education hub/corporation that will disrupt education and have a transformative impact on nations through promoting innovations, research, creativity, and entrepreneurship.

History of TIUE

TIUE was established in accordance with the Decree of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On the establishment of the international University "Tashkent International University of Education" No. 404 dated June 23, 2020.

Our Missions

Our mission is to provide an applied learning environment that allows all participants of the educational process to discover, examine critically, preserve and transmit the knowledge, wisdom and values that will help ensure the existence of this and future generations and improve the quality of life for all.

Our Values

Affordable education

High-quality and effective educational programs, training and consultations that meet the needs of students and the educational community

Supportive and collaborative relationships between faculty, students, staff, and educational programs at the University

Recognition at the local and national level as a model institution for teacher training and professional development

Gaining an international profile

Dear educators, students and Families

With a great enthusiasm I am proud to welcome you to one of the most inspirational placesnot only of learning, but also discovering, exploring, and in collaboration with highly qualified educators transforming the world- Tashkent International University of Education (TIUE).

Our University has been the eighth university that takes place in the higher education life of Uzbekistan and was established in accordance with the Decree of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On the establishment of the international University "Tashkent International University of Education" No. 404 dated June 23, 2020.

The whole world has long recognized the enormous contribution of great Central Asian philosophers to the development of world science and culture, such as Muhamad Musa al-Khwarizmi, Ahmad al-Ferghani, Abunasra al-Farabi , Abu Ali Ibn Sina , Abu RayhanBeruni. To this day, the main idea of Eastern thinkers lies at the heart of the educational process of modern Uzbekistan, which is that scientific knowledge and work contribute to the development of the best moral qualities that lead to spiritual development, improvement of the mind, consciousness, intelligence, and critical thinking.

Our primary purpose is creating an innovative ecosystem in the country by facilitating of cultivation of modern leaders in Education, Science, Government and Public services. Our mission is to provide an applied learning environment that allows all participants to discover, examine critically, preserve and transmit the knowledge, wisdom and values that will help to promote innovation, creativity, research and entrepreneurship.

TIUE was created to solve significant tasks for the present and future generations and, vertically integrating, in 2020-2023, the University will work hard in the following areas:

Higher education- Bachelor's degree in three programs- pedagogical subjects, including the STEAM education, in information and communication subjects and in business management. We provide our students full-time, part-time, evening, special correspondence and distance learning in Uzbek, English and other languages.

The amount of knowledge that our youth received during school does not meet the requirements of universities. We want to raise their educational level and further enroll not only in our University, but also in the universities of our Uzbekistan, where there are about 15 different branches of foreign universities, as well as 8 private universities.

We decided to prepare our young people for admission to Universities and created The Transformation hub for developing the mind-set and fundamental concepts underlying the four core competencies: critical thinking, creative thinking, effective communication, effective interaction.The Transformation hub provide successful admission of applicants To higher education institutions of Uzbekistan, international Universities of Near and Far Abroad, using educational programs in cooperation with established international schools and guaranteed admission of students to undergraduate programs from the 2nd year in foreign Universities of the Near and Far Abroad.

Management training-Leadership Academy is the actual solution for improving the quality of theoretical and practical knowledge and skills of administrative staff in the field of management.

Today, most of the teachers working in the secondary education system have low qualifications. During last year's certification for 2019, which was attended by almost 57 thousand teachers and mentors, 43% of those certified received low grades, and the average for the Republic was 64 points on the 100-point system. This is a clear sign of an imperfect system of professional development of teachers, which requires updating the methodological support.

Our university offers program on Professional development of teachers of secondary schools to improve the professional skills of teachers and the quality of their teaching in secondary schools.

A fresh start is always the harbinger of novelty for the institutions as well as it is for the life of people. The fast-paced and changing business world and social needs have made it necessary for our students to develop themselves individually apart from their learning areas. In this respect, it is our primary goal to build education and training programs that will enable our students in all programs to gain the competencies described as the necessities of 21st century, to make it possible for them to grow up as democratic, free-thinking, broad-minded individuals, and to create a campus environment for this purpose.

As TIUE Chancellor, I view myself as a servant leader. That is, I believe my job is to work on behalf of the entire TIUE community.

Hereby, TIUE invites students, academics and educators who believe that they can transform and bring a new breath to the development of the educational environment, learn more about our University and join our creative community.

I consider it is a great honor to serve this new, but with far-reaching goalsinstitution and its community as TIUE’s first chancellor!

With best wishes, UlugbekKasimkhadjayev TIUE Chancellor
