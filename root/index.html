About TIUE

The Tashkent International University of Education (TIUE) is a private educational institution founded in 2020 and licensed by the Government of the Republic of Uzbekistan thereof. The creation of the TIUE was based on a guiding principle and a belief that greater access to a more diversified education will contribute to a greater social justice.
The goal of providing greater access to education will be realized through a four-pillar system based on both private and public-private partnership models.

Our Vision

Tashkent International University of Education will become a purposeful education hub/corporation that will disrupt education and have a transformative impact on nations through promoting innovations, research, creativity, and entrepreneurship.

History of TIUE

TIUE was established in accordance with the Decree of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On the establishment of the international University "Tashkent International University of Education" No. 404 dated June 23, 2020.

Our Missions

Our mission is to provide an applied learning environment that allows all participants of the educational process to discover, examine critically, preserve and transmit the knowledge, wisdom and values that will help ensure the existence of this and future generations and improve the quality of life for all.
