About Us

Our University has been the eighth university that takes place in the higher education life of Uzbekistan and was established in accordance with the Decree of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On the establishment of the international University "Tashkent International University of Education" No. 404 dated June 23, 2020. Our primary purpose is creating an innovative ecosystem in the country by facilitating of cultivation of modern leaders in Education, Science, Government and Public services. Our target is to provide an applied learning environment that allows all participants to discover, examine critically, preserve and transmit the knowledge, wisdom and values that will help to promote innovation, creativity, research and entrepreneurship.

Bachelor's degree

TIUE offers three faculties in the bachelor's degree:

  • Bachelor's Degree in Business Management

    Business management program provide a broad knowledge of the various business disciplines, such as accounting, marketing, insurance, finance, administration, management science and human resource management.

  • Bachelor's Degree in Information and Communication Subjects

    Information Communication Technology students gain knowledge and skills needed to effectively apply, use and manage technology when solving problems specifically related to information and communication.

  • Bachelor's Degree in Pedagogical Disciplines (including the STEAM Education)

    STEAM is an educational approach to learning that uses Science, Technology, Engineering, the Arts and Mathematics as access points for guiding student inquiry, dialogue, and critical thinking
