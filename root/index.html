About Us

Our University has been the eighth university that takes place in the higher education life of Uzbekistan and was established in accordance with the Decree of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan "On the establishment of the international University "Tashkent International University of Education" No. 404 dated June 23, 2020. Our primary purpose is creating an innovative ecosystem in the country by facilitating of cultivation of modern leaders in Education, Science, Government and Public services. Our target is to provide an applied learning environment that allows all participants to discover, examine critically, preserve and transmit the knowledge, wisdom and values that will help to promote innovation, creativity, research and entrepreneurship.
